8.2 Discussion Forums

Discussion of the future of the Python documentation and related topics takes place in the ``Doc-SIG'' special interest group. Information on the group, including mailing list archives and subscriptions, is available at http://www.python.org/sigs/doc-sig/. The SIG is open to all interested parties.

Comments and bug reports on the standard documents should be sent to python-docs@python.org. This may include comments about formatting, content, grammatical errors, or this document.
